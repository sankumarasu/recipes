How to make grilled cheese sandwich:

Step 1: Heat a cast iron pan on stove with butter on medium heat for 3 mins

Step 2: Place two pieces of bread with Cheese of your choice in between them, until brown on one side.

Step 3: Flip to the other side and cook for 2 to 3 mins until golden brown

Tip: Add 2 slices of bacon and tomato to make it yummier
